Competitions functionalities (WORK IN PROGRESS)

Last updated: Dec 13, 2020

Important

You must be logged in as an admin to access table functionalities!

Competitions Overview

To get an overview of all the tables, click on 'Manage items' in the navigation tab, and click on 'Competitions' (or click here).
The navigation tab is located top-left of your screen.
This will show you each competition, with its name.

Create competition

When you want to create a new competition, please follow these steps:

  1. You start on the competitions overview page (see above).
  2. On the top-right of the list, you see a green arrow with the text: 'New'. Click on it.
  3. On the page that follows, fill in the competition details.
  4. Click save.
  5. That's it!

Edit competition

Editing a competition is easy if you follow these steps:

  1. At the end of a row, you will see this icon: more_vert. Click on it.
  2. A pop-up will show with the following text: 'Edit competition'. Click on it.
  3. You are now on the selected competition's edit page!
  4. Edit the fields as you like, and click save.
  5. You're done!

Delete competition

Deleteing a competition is easy if you follow these steps:

  1. At the end of a row, you will see this icon: more_vert. Click on it.
  2. A pop-up will show with the following text: 'Delete competition'. Click on it.
  3. Click confirm
  4. You're done!
