Team Functionalities

Last updated: Dec 13, 2020

Important

You must be logged in to access team functionalities!

Teams overview

To get an overview of all the teams, click on 'Teams' in the navigation tab (or click here).
The navigation tab is located top-left of your screen.
This will show you each team, with their name, for which company they work, where they are located,
who their captain is and what their status is.

Team details page

If you want to know certain details about a team, you can find them by following these steps:

  1. At the end of a row, you will see this icon: more_vert. Click on it.
  2. A pop-up will show with the following text: 'Team Details'. Click on it.
  3. That's it! You are now on the selected team's detail page!

Team profile picture

Each team on this application has a profile picture. To change this, follow these steps.

  1. Start on your team details page (see above).
  2. Move your cursor to the profile picture.
  3. The picture will change to a green square with a white pencil inside it. Click it.
  4. A modal will open, with a green button saying 'Choose file'. Click it.
  5. Now you can choose a picture on your computer with windows explorer.
  6. Select the file, and click open (or double-click on the file).
  7. Click save.
  8. That's it! Not too shabby eh?

You can always cancel by clicking the cancel button.

Important

Only the team captain can access this feature!

Join team

For those players who like playing in team, but don't like to have the captain's responsibility:
This one is for you!
Follow these steps to join a team:

  1. You start on a team's detail page (see above).
  2. Next to the team's image and under the team's name, you'll see a shiny green button.
  3. If you want to join this specific team, click on it.
  4. Now the button turns yellow, and says that your request is sent.
  5. If you chosen team's captain accepts you, you're in!
  6. If not, find another team to take your revenge!

Leave team

If, for any reason, you would like to leave a team, you're out of luck!
Just kidding, of course you can. Just follow these steps:

  1. Start on the team's detail page.
  2. Next to the team's image, and under the team's name, you'll see a red button. Click it.
  3. There you go! You just left that team! Easy right?

Create team

Every user can make as much teams as they want. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. You start on the teams overview page (see above).
  2. On the top-right of the list, you see a green arrow with the text: 'New'. Click on it.
  3. On the page that follows, fill in the team details.
  4. Click save.
  5. Congratulations! You are now the captain of your very own team!

Important

An admin can access this feature via Manage items -> teams in the navigation tab!

Edit team

As a team captain, you can edit the information of your team. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. You start on the teams overview page (see above).
  2. Next to the team picture, you'll see a green button 'edit team'. Click it.
  3. Here you can manage the team's information and approve or decline requests.
  4. Click save.
  5. Your team has now been edited!

Important

Only the team captain can access this feature!

An admin can also access this feature via
Manage items -> teams -> more_vert -> edit team in the navigation tab!

Disband team

As a team captain, you decide to disband your team. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. You start on the teams overview page (see above).
  2. Click the red button saying 'disband team'.
  3. Confirm your choice.
  4. Your team has now been disbanded! See ya!

Important

Only the team captain can access this feature!

An admin can also access this feature via
Manage items -> teams -> more_vert -> delete team in the navigation tab!

Change team status

As a team captain, change your team's status. First, let's explain these statuses:

  • Open: any user can join your team, without sending a request.
  • Review: the captain decides who joins the team. Requests are necessary to approve joining users.
  • Closed: the team doesn't accept new users. Incoming requests are disabled.

To change your team's status, follow these steps:

  1. You start on the teams overview page (see above).
  2. Click the green button saying 'change status'.
  3. Select your choice and confirm.
  4. Your team's status has now been updated.

Important

Only the team captain can access this feature!
