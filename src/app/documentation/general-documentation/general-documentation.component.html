General Information

Last updated: Dec 13, 2020

What is SoccerCAST?

SoccerCAST is a web application, made to make organising matches, competitions and tournaments for tablesoccer.
It has functions for:

  • User management
  • Team management
  • Table management
  • Organising matches, competitions and tournaments
  • Tracking statistics

and more!

Why did you make SoccerCAST?

Each year, the Grasshoppers Academy organises an application challenge for our faculty.
This year (2020), they asked us to make an application with the subject of tablesoccer.
The initial pitch said that they wanted to use the application with companies inside their group, but it should be possible for everyone to join. The challenge started on Monday Dec 7, 2020. During the first two days, the teams could ask experts from jstack and Qframe questions about best-practices and general tips
for the front- and back-end of the application.

How did you make SoccerCAST?

Introduction

Each web application has front-end software (the pages the user sees), and back-end software
(handles data exchange between front-end and database).
Here you can read which technologies we used.

Front-end

Our front-end is made with the Angular framework, made by Google.
The lay-out we used was a mix from:

  • Base lay-out: Bootstrap
  • Iconography: Material design
  • Documentation lay-out by: Xiaoying Riley
  • Tournament brackets: jQuery Bracket
  • And our own style.

Back-end

We made our back-end with the .NET framework, made by Microsoft.
To store data, we used a MySQL database. We used Blob storage to store images.

Hosting

To host our application online, we used two services:

  • Firebase: hosting our front-end
  • Microsoft Azure: hosting our back-end and our databases.
