Matches functionalities

Last updated: Dec 13, 2020

Important

  • You must be logged in to use this feature!
  • These features only work with singe matches, not tournament matches!

Challenge a user or team

When you want to plan a match, please follow these steps:

  1. You start on a user's/team's detail page.
  3. A modal appears, fill in the match details.

    Challenge a team

    • Select a soccer table
    • Select your team
    • Select player/user 1 of your team
    • Select player/user 2 of your team
    • Click challenge

    Challenge a user

    • Select a soccer table
    • Click challenge
  4. That's it!

Matchrequests

When you want to accept or decline a challenge, please follow these steps:

  1. You start on your user's/team's detail page.
  2. Click on 'REQUESTS'.
  3. On the row of the specific challenge you want to handle, click this icon: more_vert
  4. Click accept or decline
  5. Your answer has now been handled.

Start a match

If your challenge was accepted and you want to start a match, please follow these steps:

  1. You start on your user's/team's detail page.
  2. Click on 'PLANNED'.
  3. On the row of the specific match you want to play, click this icon: more_vert
  4. Click start match.

Important

You must be the challenger to update scores!

Cancel match

To cancel a match, there are two options:

  1. You decline the request.
  2. You cancel the match after you accept. Follow these steps:
    • You start on your user's page.
    • Click on 'PLANNED'.
    • On the row of the specific match you want to cancel, click this icon: more_vert
    • Click cancel match.

You have now cancelled a match!

Match scores

While you play a match, the challenger can update the scores, use these methods:

  • Click the green '+' button to add 1 point to a score.
  • Click the red '-' button to subtract 1 point from a score.

Important

You must be the challenger to update scores!

Stop a match

While you play a match, or if you've had enough use this method to end a match:

  • Click the red 'Stop match' button to stop the match

Important

  • Only the challenger/cretor of the match can end the match!
  • After you've stopped a match, there is no way of restarting it!
