Who is SoccerCast?

SoccerCast is the name of an application of a group of enthusiastic students in their 3rd year of the IT Factory.

SoccerCast is a Web application that makes it possible for companies to organize soccer table matches. They can challange each other and view there statistics.

The team of SoccerCast made this project during the application chalange.
