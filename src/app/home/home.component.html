What is SoccerCast?

SoccerCast is a web application created by a group of enthusiastic students in their 3rd year of the IT Factory @ Thomas More in Geel.

With SoccerCast, companies have the possibility to organize table soccer matches. They can challenge each other and view their statistics.

The team of SoccerCast made this project during the Grasshoppers Academy application challenge 2020.
